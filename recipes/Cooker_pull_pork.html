Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Ingredients

  • 1 (2 pound) pork tenderloin
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle root beer
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle your favorite barbecue sauce
    • 8 hamburger buns, split and lightly toasted

Directions

  1. Gather the Ingredients
  2. Place pork tenderloin in a slow cooker; pour root beer over top
  3. Cover ad cook on Low until pork shreds easily, 6 to 7 hours.Notel the acutal length of time may vary according to the individual slow cooker .
  4. Drain well. Stir in barbecue Sauce.
  5. Serve on hamburger buns
  6. Server and Enjoy !
